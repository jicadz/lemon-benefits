A Glass of Water with a Slice of Lemon

Lemon water is a refreshing beverage made by mixing fresh lemon juice with water. It is known for its tangy flavor and numerous health benefits. Drinking water with lemon is an easy way to enhance hydration, support digestion, and give your immune system a natural boost. This simple ritual can invigorate your day and contribute to overall wellness.
